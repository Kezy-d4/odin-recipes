Grilled Chicken Shawarma Wrap

At a glance

Shawarma is the delectable middle-eastern method of preparing various meats, in this case, chicken. Traditionally, shawarma meat is slowly cooked on a vertical rotating rotisserie spit which produces heavenly bits of fatty, moist meat. In this recipe, we will instead be pan-grilling the chicken, making it easier to prepare at home and still tasting fantastic!

Chicken shawarma can be enjoyed in various ways, such as: on a salad, over fries, on its own, or as we'll be doing in this recipe, stuffed in a tasty and filling wrap!

Ingredients

  • Chicken & Marinade:
    • Large chicken breasts
    • Plain yogurt
    • garlic, minced
    • lemon juice
    • cinnamon
    • dried oregano
    • salt
    • ground nutmeg
    • ground cloves
  • Tzatziki Sauce:
    • plain greek yogurt
    • garlic, minced
    • dried dill
    • salt
  • Wrap:
    • Naan, pita, or flatbread
    • Romaine lettuce leaves
    • cucumber, sliced into pieces
    • roma tomato, sliced into pieces

Step by step

  1. In a bowl, combine the ingredients for the marinade (yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, cinnamon, oregano, salt, nutmeg, and clove). Slice the chicken breasts into strips and add them to the marinade. Mix and coat well, cover and refrigerate for 4-24 hours.
  2. Prepare the tzatziki sauce for the wrap. Mix the plain greek yogurt, garlic, dill, and salt and stir well. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  3. Pan grill the marinated chicken in a drop of olive oil until cooked through.
  4. Prepare the vegetables for the wrap. Wash and chop the tomato, lettuce, and cucumber.
  5. Build the chicken shawarma wrap. Spread the tzatziki sauce on your bread, top with the chicken and vegetables, and roll the sandwich closed.
  6. Serve with a side of fries, salad, or enjoy on its own!
