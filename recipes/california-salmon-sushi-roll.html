California Salmon Sushi Roll

Description

This California salmon sushi roll is stuffed with a mouthwatering combination of imitation crab, crunchy cucumber, and creamy avocado. It is then topped with a succulent slice of fresh salmon. This roll is sure to deliver the perfect experience with each bite and can easily be made at home.

Ingredients

  • Imitation crab
  • Sushi-grade salmon, sliced into strips
  • Sushi rice, cool
  • Nori sheet
  • Cucumber, sliced into strips
  • Avocado, sliced into strips
  • Toasted sesame seeds (optional)
