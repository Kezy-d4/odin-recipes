California Salmon Sushi Roll

At a glance

The California roll is a simple and classic sushi roll. Premium versions of the roll may use real crab meat, though most often it is prepared with imitation crab meat (surimi sticks), and this is how we will prepare it in this recipe.

This California salmon sushi roll is stuffed with a mouthwatering combination of imitation crab meat, crunchy cucumber, and creamy avocado. It is then topped with succulent fresh salmon. This roll is sure to deliver the perfect experience with each bite and can easily be made at home.

Ingredients

  • Imitation crab meat
  • Sashimi-grade salmon, sliced into strips
  • Sushi rice, cool
  • Nori sheet
  • Cucumber, sliced into strips
  • Avocado, sliced into strips
  • Optional:
    • Toasted sesame Seeds
    • Spicy Mayonnaise
    • Ponzu sauce

Step by step

  1. Lay the nori sheet flat on a bamboo sushi rolling mat, rough side facing up.
  2. Wet your hands, and spread the sushi rice thinly across the nori sheet.
  3. If you wish, sprinkle toasted sesame seeds across the rice.
  4. Flip the nori sheet over on the rolling mat.
  5. Place the imitation crab meat sticks, cucumber strips, and avocado strips towards the lower half of the nori sheet.
  6. Ensuring the nori sheet is on the edge of the rolling mat towards you, carefully roll and form the sushi roll.
  7. Using a sharp and damp knife, slice any excess fillings off the ends of the roll, and slice into 8 even pieces.
  8. Lay a strip of fresh salmon just over the top of each piece.
  9. If you wish, drizzle spicy mayonnaise and/or ponzu sauce over the top of the pieces.
  10. Serve either on its own, or accompanied by ginger, wasabi, and soy sauce.
  11. Enjoy!
