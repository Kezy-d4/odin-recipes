Traditional Banana Split

Description

The banana split is a classic no-compromise American treat. It consists of a peeled banana sliced in half lengthwise and filled with scoops of ice cream and various sweet toppings, such as: candies, sweet sauces, whipped cream, nuts, fruits, and more.

Our version of the recipe will fill the split with three scoops of ice cream and some simple toppings, but one of the key characteristics of the banana split is its customizability, so feel free to fill your banana split with whichever and however much ice cream and toppings your heart desires!

Ingredients

  • Large ripe banana
  • Several varieties of ice cream
  • Fresh cherries
  • Crushed walnuts
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Fresh strawberries
  • Crushed pineapple
